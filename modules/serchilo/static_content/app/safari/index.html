The Safari extension integrates Serchilo easily into Safari 6.

  • It adds Serchilo to the address bar
  • and allows customization in the extensions's preferences:

Install

  1. Check if you are using at least Safari 6. (Safari > About)
  2. Download the extension.
  3. Click on the downloaded file serchilo.safariextz.
  4. Click on Open.
  5. Click on Install.

Usage

  • Just type in your Serchilo commands into the address bar (don't get distracted by "Google search" appearing).
  • Change settings at Safari > Preferences > Extensions > Serchilo
