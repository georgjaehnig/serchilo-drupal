Make [site:name] the default search

  1. Go to your [site:name] homepage.

  2. In the footer, click on Add to browser (next to Impressum) and confirm.

  3. Right click in the address bar and select Edit search engines.

  4. In Other search engines, find [site:name].

  5. Point your mouse on that (added) line. A button Make default will appear. Click on it.

  6. Done. Now you can type [site:name] queries directly into your address field.
